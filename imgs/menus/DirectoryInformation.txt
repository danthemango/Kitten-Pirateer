Within this directory is the various menu's and prompt images that are used with in
the game.

These images were modified and/or created by Keenan Longair.
Any comments, suggestions or ideas to help make these images more interesting and
engaging can be passed along to him at any time. He is no expert at graphics, but 
is happy for help.

Within the old sub-directory, the images previously used are stored. These may be 
cleaned out once they are deemed unnecessary.

Within the resources sub-directory, the xcf files are located for editing of these
images when necessary. These will be updated everytime a change occurs.
